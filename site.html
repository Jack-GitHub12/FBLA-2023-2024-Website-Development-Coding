Dental Hygienist

As a Dental Hygienist at FBLA Dentistry, you'll have the opportunity to work alongside our dental team to provide excellent oral health care. Responsibilities include teeth cleaning, examining patients for signs of oral diseases such as gingivitis, and providing other preventive dental care. 70-85k/year

Orthodontic Assistant

The Orthodontic Assistant supports orthodontists in patient care, helping with the adjustment of orthodontic appliances and providing patient education on oral hygiene specific to orthodontic treatment. 90-95k/year

Office Manager

The Office Manager will oversee the daily operations of our dental office, ensuring the efficiency of our services, managing staff, and handling administrative tasks to facilitate smooth and effective office functioning. 100-115k/year
